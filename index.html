Rules of the game

Ping pong ought to count to your number in ascending order from 1 wand will make the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers that are divisible by 3 become 'ping'
  2. Numbers that are divisible by 5 become 'pong'
  3. Numbers that are divisible by 3 and 5 become 'PingPong'
